Loop Heat Pipe System

In this project a solution for desktop computer cooling is created via designing, manufacturing, and testing of loop heat pipe systems. Heat source, heat spreader, retention mechanism, evaporator, condenser, and pipes are all designed by cad and wick structure and fan are also added. Technical analyses and experimental analyses were done and compared as a result. Besides that, some variables like filling ratio, saturation temperature, case temperature, heat rate, and air flow rate are also calibrated on a broad spectrum.

The Beast: Rowing boat

The term project which has called "the beast" is a sample of team work and effort on the second term of freshman year. Initially, "the beast" is a simple modified rowing boat of Mike Sheppard's "the beast" which has a mechanism of John Fitch's steamboat. The building process of rowing boat consists of three basical phases: computer-aided design of keel of boat, obtaining RC motor, and mounting the system with oars. The underlying mechanism at the core of the project is converting circular motion to linear motion, linkage bar system.
